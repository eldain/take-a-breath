  • - Relax your neck and shoulder muscles

  • - Pucker your lips as if you were going to whistle
  • - Exhale through the pursed-lips for four seconds

  • - Standing up straight, expel all the air from your lungs
  • - Slowly breath in expanding your lungs to the maximum comfortable capacity and hold for 20 seconds
  • - While holding breath, rest both hands on your hips, thumbs facing forward and pinkies facing the back
  • - Then release the air slowly after the 20 seconds
  • - Repeat this exercise three more times for maximum benefit
