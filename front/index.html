Breathing Exercises
made simple

Breathing exercises have been scientifically proven to help people reduce anxiety, sleep better, improve focus, and increase lung capacity.

With our breath pacer, you can finally take advantage of these time-tested techniques and live a calmer life. Whether that means treating your COPD or simply finding a moment of peace in a stressful day, we have a breathing exercise just for you.


Our Exercises:

Box Breathing

The breathing exercise designed by Navy Seals to calm down in moments of high stress.

Pursed Lips

Developed with COPD patients in mind, this breathing technique has been proven to help patients exercise longer.

Rib Stretch

This advanced diaphragmatic breathing technique used to be reserved for yogis with years of experience, but with our breath pacer, anyone can achieve the calming benefits from this exercise.

Belly Breathing

The diaphragm is the main muscle of breathing. It’s supposed to do most of the work. When you have COPD, the diaphragm doesn’t work as well and muscles in the neck, shoulders and back are used. This exercises was designed to strengthen the diaphram.
